Let us go back to our childhood.
When we had a choice of studying Vs playing, what did we opt for?
And when we did spend our time playing, did we just have pure fun, or did we also learn things?
I am sure you all agree that play was much more than fun. It provided an opportunity to interact with others, learn in a safe and a comfortable environment. As adults, does this change? No, not much. How many of you look forward to team building sessions and outings Vs formal training sessions?
And yet the common misconception is play and work are opposites. Let us try and debunk this myth. These stories could be a starting point.
